About Dr. Fei Fei Li

Background

Dr. Fei-Fei Li is a proud innovator in the artificial intelligence industry and machine learning. She was born in Beijing, China in 1976, but moved to the United States when she was 12. She went to Princeton University, where she got her undergraduate degree in Physics while studying other areas in STEM. She went to grad school at the California Institute of Technology and got her PhD degree in electrical engineering.


She is well known for her work on The ImageNet project, this project continues to improve efficiency using AI. Her work not only shapes the future of AI but encourages the future of innovators faced with challenges. Dr. Fei-Fei Li is renowned for her groundbreaking work in AI and computer vision.
