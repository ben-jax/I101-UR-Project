About Dr. Fei Fei Li

▲Background

Dr. Fei-Fei Li is a proud innovator in the artificial intelligence industry and machine learning. She was born in Beijing, China in 1976, but moved to the United States when she was 12. She went to Princeton University, where she got her undergraduate degree in Physics while studying other areas in STEM. She went to grad school at the California Institute of Technology and got her PhD degree in electrical engineering.

She is well known for her work on The ImageNet project, this project continues to improve efficiency using AI. Her work not only shapes the future of AI but encourages the future of innovators faced with challenges. Dr. Fei-Fei Li is renowned for her groundbreaking work in AI and computer vision.


▼Contributions

ImageNet

ImageNet is a large scale visual database, that has been hugely impactful in deep learning, but more specifically computer vision research. This includes advancements in object recogniztion and visual understanding, which has big implication in major industries, including automotive, healthcare and security.

Dr. Fei Fei Li set up ImageNet in 2006, at time when most researchers in the field of artificial intellgence put more importance on algorithms than readily available datasets. Dr. Li saw a need to to for these data sets, and was convinced it would make the algorithms more efficient.

After meeting researcher Christiane Fellbaum from Princeton University who was a researcher for WorldNet, Dr. Li decided to use the WorldNet wordbase and heirarchy to launch ImageNet. It has now grown to contain over 14 million annoated images for reseachers everywhere.

Learn more about ImageNet at their website.


AI4ALL

Dr. Fei Fei Li is a co-founder and board member of AI4ALL a group that is focused on the inclusivitiy, representation, social good and better artificial intellgence. They advocate for diversity in the computer science feild and more specifically the development of artificial intellgence, as without diversity AI models can not accurately reflect society large.

AI4ALL's vision also revolves around efficiently using AI to solve social problems to imporve society. The way that they are achieving these goals is by teaching students to solve problems with innovation, and opening the doors between students and researchers all over the globe.

Learn more about AI4ALL at their website.

▼Now

Dr. Fei Fei Li is continuing her work as the Sequoia Capital professor of computer science at Standford University, as well as being the co-director of the Standford Vision and Learning Lab. From 2013 - 2018 she was the director of Standford Artificilal Intelligence Labratory, highlighting where knowledge in the fields of computer science, computer vision and artificial intellgence.

Just recently in 2023 she was listed in the Time 100 most AI most influential people list. She has also recieved countless awards including the Intel Lifetime Achievement of Innovation Award. Currently serving on the United Nations Scientific Advisory Board is one of her most impressive achievments, highlighting her ability to share her knowledge of artificial intellgence, medicine and computer science for good while driving inclusivitiy and diversity.
